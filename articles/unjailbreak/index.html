How to Unjailbreak Unc0ver

By Alexander Davis

July 1, 2022 | Estimated reading time: 18 seconds

  1. Launch the Unc0ver app
  2. Tap on the settings icon in the top left of the screen
  3. Swipe down and enable "Restore RootFS" and "Refresh Icon Cache toggles"
  4. Tap the "Done" button
  5. Press the "Restore RootFS" button
  6. Once you receive an alert along the lines of "RootFS has been successfully restored", tap the OK button to reboot your device

And there you have it! You should now be unjailbroken.
