The Case Against Coke

By Alexander Davis

You're probably familiar with the popular soft drink Coca-Cola. Coke claims that its products aren't addictive, but a quick Google search [1] [2] [3] proves otherwise. People range from seldom consuming it to drinking extreme amounts such as ten a day. This article intends to act as a warning and reminder for heavy consumers of Coke to stop drinking it.
